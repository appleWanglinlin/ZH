Garmin TT15 Mini Dog Collar GPS Tracking and Training!

Lighter and smaller than the TT 15 with a 3/4 inch polyurethane-coated collar strap, the Garmin TT 15 Mini Dog Device Collar is designed for smaller hunting dogs like beagles and lets you add more dogs to your existing Tracking & Training Sytem Alpha 100 GPS transmitter (up to 20 dogs). The TT 15 mini is also compatible with Astro 430 and 320 for tracking mode only.


The TT 15 mini collar tracking and training dog device with collar is tough enough to run wherever your dog runs, up to 4 miles away. A top-mounted, high-sensitivity GPS and GLONASS satellite receiver for safe, secure and more effective tracking, even where Internet and cell phone service isn’t available. Rugged and water-resistant up to 10 meters.


The stimulation features let you train each dog separately, traditional stimulation allows you to make quick changes (Low, Medium or High) within a predetermined stimulation level (1-6), whereas linear stimulation allows progressively more stimulation from levels 1 to 18. You can remotely operate the LED beacon lights, built in to the dog device, to help locate your dog in low-light conditions or at night.


TT 15 Mini ships with a 3/4-inch red collar strap + One Free Extra collar strap included with 10 colors to choose from.


Note: The TT15 Mini is only compatible with the Alpha 100, PRO 550 PLUS, Astro 430, and the Astro 320 . TT 15 mini can be configured for operation with either handheld but cannot be tracked simultaneously by both the Astro 430 and 320 at the same time.

Features:

  • Range up to 4 Mile
  • For smaller dogs 10 pounds and up and perfect for all breeds and coat thickness
  • High-sensitivity GPS/GLONASS receiver
  • VHF Antenna steel braided for rough abuse in field
  • GPS Antenna TIP is orange and easy to see
  • Red collar strap adjustable & replaceable 3/4 Inch (1.9 cm)
  • Neck size 9.5” to 24”
  • Dimensions of receiver collar: 3.1" x 1.8” x 1.4" (7.8 x 4.5 x 3.5 cm)
  • Weight: 5.6 oz (158 g) without collar strap
  • Rescue mode if in event battery becomes low conserving battery life
  • Receiver collar low battery indicator light
  • Beacon light for low visibility & light conditions
  • 18 levels of continuous and 18 momentary stimulation
  • Vibration & Tone
  • USB Interface
  • Collar Sleep Mode; Save battery life in the dog device collar-put it in "sleep mode" from the transmitter
  • Battery life between charges 16-30 hours depending on update rate
  • Waterproof Rating 1ATM withstands pressures equal to a depth of 32 feet (10 meters)
  • Rechargeable and replaceable Li-ion battery pack
  • Rugged and waterproof the system withstands dust, dirt, humidity or water
